Scotland has a lot to offer, especially if you have your own way of transport.
Here, we propose some lovely hikes all within a 2-hour drive from Glasgow.

The Devil's Pulpit and the Whangie

Getting to the Devil's Pulpit, takes about a thirty minutes from Glasgow. The gorge real name is Finnich Glen, but is generally known as the Devil's Pulpit due to the red-tinted water of a rock formation which resembles a church pulpit.
