Edinbugh




There are many ways of getting to the beautiful city of Edinburgh, but we propose you give cycling it a try. The cycle takes you along the Forth and Clyde to then continue into the Union canal. It is more than 60 miles of traffic-free, flat towpath, an absolute delight of a daytrip. Besides, you can come and go in a day as it will only take you a couple of hours to get from city to city.
